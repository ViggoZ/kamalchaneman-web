Hood

  • Interaction Design
  • Product Design
  • UI/UX

Hood is a social commerce product ideas that came up from one of the respected indonesian fashion designer. Over the years, he has attracted thousand of fans and followers in Indonesia. His works is designed for people who live life on their terms and want to look good with it.

Through a very collaborative effort, we help him to created and designed it’s web and mobile app with an emphasis on brings content and commerce together. With an aggressive timeline, a sizeable amount of work and ambitious growth goals, we were excited to put our minds together and get started to solve this problems.

I was worked with a team consist of 1 project manager, 1 lead designer, 2 UI Designer and 3 developer. I was tasked with creating a new UI for hood mobile app as well as designing the UX from scratch, prototyping and interaction.

ProblemsThe goal of hood is to allow users to discover and interact with other users and experts based on their preferred style. Hood wanted to be an ecommerce app that bring a human element and personality to the users, also wanted users to be stylish and stay ahead of trends.

Once we aligned on the goals, we set ourselves up as an extension of his team and conducted research and working sessions to define key considerations.

One of the big challenges I faced as the mobile designer of the app was to diferentiate from other competitors while still creating simple yet engaging product.

SolutionsAfter having an initial conversation with client, team, piloting the MVP and submitting the client ideas to solve the problems, I start gathered the necessary resources, I went straight to the drawing board and began researching competitors and sketching out ideas.

WireframingAt the beginning of the process we start wireframing to help me get out ideas in a quick and dirty fashion so we could discuss the design as a team.

User InterfaceThe main creative direction I was given is to understand the workflow and design a base UI that could be iterated upon at a later stage. Designing a visual language that was going to be used consistently throughout the app. The UI is built to ensure the user never gets lost by guiding them with instant feedback during the start, middle and end of each action.

IterationOne of the great things about this product is that the team and I continued to iterate very quickly so that we could increase engagement. We analyzed data and user feedback to make add new features and enhancements to the product every week.

ReflectionThere were definitely challenges with designing this app. There are a lot of moving parts to the hood product. Things may not be perfect with this app given the design and engineering constraints we had. However, the numbers just don’t lie. Users testing proved they are loving the app and returning again and again to discover and share content with each other.

Even though the app is postponed due to client internal problem, I consider it a success based on what I’ve heard from users who had tested the app. The feedback has been very positive and received quite a bit of praise for the intuitiveness of the UI and UX.

It’s been a team e ort that has required constant communication. To make peoples lives better through design, even if it’s just a little bit, is why I do what I do.
