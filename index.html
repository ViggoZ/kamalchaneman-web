I’m a self-taught Indonesian UI/UX designer for digital products, fuelled with curiosity and passion for simplicity in design.

I am excited by smart mark-up, clever css and pixel perfection. When I’m not designing, I spend time with my family, playing football and reading.

Indonesian UI/UX designer

Over the past 6 years I have worked for various companies, startups and personal clients conceptualizing and defining overall design direction including creation of sketches, wireframes, hi-fidelity designs and prototypes. Coordinates with developers to make sure the implementation is aligned with design process.

I have worked with multiple disciplines as well as other related design disciplines including interaction design, user interface design and user experience design.

I love a challenge. I love the idea of working with people that are brilliant.
